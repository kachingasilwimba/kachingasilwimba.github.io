Featured AI & ML Projects

AI-Powered EOF Analysis in CLM5

Built Python-based AI pipelines using Empirical Orthogonal Functions to extract spatiotemporal soil moisture patterns across CONUS, leveraging HPC clusters for large-scale geospatial data processing.

EOF + SOM Geospatial Clustering

Integrated EOF decomposition with Self-Organizing Maps (unsupervised ML) to classify soil moisture regimes, unveiling regional sensitivities and improving model interpretability through advanced clustering algorithms.

EDNN Deep Learning Emulation of CLM5 LAI

Developed Evidential Deep Neural Networks for rapid Leaf Area Index emulation, achieving R² > 0.98 and quantifying both aleatoric and epistemic uncertainties in real-time predictions.

Surrogate ML for Hydrological Forecasting

Deployed surrogate AI models using perturbed-parameter ensembles to forecast streamflow and terrestrial water storage in ungauged basins, combining physical insights with deep learning and uncertainty quantification.
